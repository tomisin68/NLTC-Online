Quick Note

When you save your cookie choice on our site, we may store that preference so we do not keep asking you on every page load.

1. What Cookies Are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, tags, or other tools used to remember settings, keep sessions secure, or understand how a service is performing.

2. How NLTC Uses Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use storage technologies to keep the platform functioning properly, remember preferences, support secure sign-in, maintain session continuity, and improve reliability. Where optional analytics or preference tools are enabled, we aim to give you a meaningful choice before using them.

3. Cookie Categories We May Use

Category Purpose Typical Example Consent Position
Strictly necessary Support account login, security checks, load balancing, session continuity, and core platform functions. Session identifiers, authentication state, security tokens. Usually enabled because the site relies on them to work.
Preference Remember user choices like cookie settings, accessibility preferences, or interface behavior. Saved cookie preference or display setting. Used based on your choice or service necessity.
Performance and analytics Help us understand usage patterns, reliability, and feature effectiveness. Aggregated analytics or diagnostic tools, if enabled. Only where we choose to enable them and you allow them.
Third-party service support Support payment flows, embedded content, or linked external services. Payment processor or media provider storage. Depends on the service and your interaction with it.

4. Third-Party Services

Some third-party providers connected to NLTC may use their own cookies or similar technologies when you interact with their tools. Examples may include payment providers, hosted authentication services, or embedded content partners. Their privacy and cookie practices are governed by their own policies.

5. Managing Your Choices

You can manage cookie-related choices in several ways:

  • Use the cookie banner on the NLTC website to choose between broader consent and essential-only use.
  • Adjust your browser settings to block, delete, or limit cookies.
  • Clear relevant site storage from your device if you want to remove saved preferences.
  • Contact us if you have a question about a cookie or a storage technology used on the service.
Important: blocking essential cookies may affect sign-in, secure sessions, payment continuity, or key learning features.

6. Retention of Cookie Preferences

We may keep a record of your cookie preference for a reasonable period so that the site remembers your choice and does not repeatedly request the same consent decision on every visit.

7. Updates and Contact

We may update this Cookie Policy as the website evolves, especially if we add new tools or categories of storage technology. When we make material changes, we will revise the effective date and update the website notice where appropriate.

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@nltc.ng.
